Amaury Guichon

His control over chocolate is out of this world!

Amaury Guichon is a French pastry chef and chocolatier who is well-known for his incredible pastry creations, particularly his intricately designed chocolate sculptures. He was born on May 15, 1984, in Marseille, France.

Guichon began his culinary career at the age of 16, when he started an apprenticeship at a local bakery. He later moved to Paris to study at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure de Pâtisserie, where he honed his skills in pastry making and chocolate work.

After completing his studies, Guichon worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, including the Burj Al Arab hotel in Dubai, the Ritz Carlton hotel in Moscow, and the Mandarin Oriental hotel in Geneva. In 2013, he moved to Las Vegas to work at the Jean Philippe Patisserie at the Bellagio hotel.

Guichon has won several awards throughout his career, including the prestigious Meilleur Ouvrier de France (Best Craftsman of France) award in 2018. He is also a popular figure on social media, with over 2 million followers on Instagram, where he shares photos and videos of his amazing pastry creations.
